Animal Transformations

Caterpillar to Butterfly

The transformation from a caterpillar to a butterfly is one of the most fascinating processes in nature. The caterpillar forms a chrysalis and undergoes metamorphosis to emerge as a beautiful butterfly.

Tadpole to Frog

A tadpole starts its life in water, breathing through gills. Over time, it develops legs, loses its tail, and transforms into a frog, capable of living both in water and on land.

Larva to Beetle

Beetles undergo complete metamorphosis, starting as larvae, then pupating, and finally emerging as adult beetles. This process can take weeks to years, depending on the species.
